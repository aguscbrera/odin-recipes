Chow fan

Description

Chow fan is a popular Chinese dish made with stir-fried rice and eggs, vegetables, and often meat or tofu. The rice is typically cooked until fluffy and then stir-fried with a variety of ingredients, resulting in a savory and satisfying meal.

It is a versatile dish that can be customized with various ingredients, making it a favorite among many.

Ingredients

  • 200gr of chow fan rice (or jasmine rice)
  • 3 eggs
  • Half carrot
  • A cup of green beans
  • Handful of green onion
  • Handful of soy sprouts
  • Soy sauce
  1. Boil the rice according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Slice the carrot into small dice and chop the green part of the green onion.
  3. Heat a large pan or wok over medium-high heat and add a bit of oil.
  4. Beat the eggs in a bowl and pour them into the pan, scrambling them until half cooked.
  5. Add the cooked rice to the pan and stir to combine well.
  6. Add the diced carrot and green beans, stir-frying for about 2-3 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.
  7. Add the chopped green onion and soy sauce to taste. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  8. Season with salt and pepper as desired.
  9. Finally, add the soy sprouts and stir-fry for an additional minute.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chow fan!
