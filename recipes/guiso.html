Guiso

Description

Guiso de lentejas is a hearty and nutritious stew made with lentils, vegetables, and often meat. It is a traditional dish in many cultures, known for its comforting flavors and filling nature.

This dish is perfect for cold days and is often served with bread or rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of lentils
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 potatoes, diced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 4 cups of vegetable or chicken broth
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil for cooking
  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat some olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onion and garlic, saut√©ing until the onion is translucent.
  4. Add the diced carrots, potatoes, and bell pepper, cooking for about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the lentils and pour in the broth. Bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for about 30-40 minutes, or until the lentils are tender.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot, optionally with bread or rice on the side.
  9. Enjoy your delicious guiso de lentejas!
