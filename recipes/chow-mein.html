Chow mein

Description

Chow mein is a popular Chinese dish made with stir-fried noodles, vegetables, and often meat or tofu. The noodles are typically thin and crispy, and the dish is known for its savory flavor and satisfying texture.

It is a versatile dish that can be customized with various ingredients, making it a favorite among many.

Ingredients

  • 200gr of chow mein noodles (or egg noodles)
  • Half red onion
  • Half carrot
  • Half red bell pepper
  • 1/4 green cabbage
  • Handful of green onion
  • Handful of soy sprouts
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  2. Slice the vegetables thinly.
  3. Heat a large pan or wok over medium-high heat and add a bit of oil.
  4. Add the onions and carrots, stir-frying for about 2 minutes.
  5. Add the bell pepper, cabbage, and green onion, cooking for another 2-3 minutes until they are tender but still crisp.
  6. Add the cooked noodles to the pan, along with soy sauce to taste. Stir well to combine all ingredients.
  7. Finally, add the soy sprouts and stir-fry for an additional minute.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade chow mein!
